I had the chance to join a 3-day **hackathon** at my university, where we developed **machine learning** models to predict movie ratings on **IMDB**. During the event, I learned new skills such as **image processing** and gained deeper understanding of **AI models**.

---

## The hackathon had two phases

1. ### **Movie rating prediction**
   The first phase focused on predicting a movie score based on its features (actors, category, year, duration, etc.). We were given a dataset of around 10,000 movies and had to train our models with it.

2. ### **Prediction from movie posters**
   The second phase was similar, but using **movie posters**. Here, **image processing** became key. My approach was to analyze image **pixels** and extract the three most frequent colors.

Even though the final results were not top-ranked, it was an excellent learning experience.

---

The winners from our venue, who reached the **European final**, showed an impressive level. It was exhausting but also **very rewarding**.
