## Websites I use regularly

- [Thingiverse](https://www.thingiverse.com/): Huge collection of printable **3D models**.
- [Gulag Cleaner](https://gulagcleaner.com/): Removes abusive **ads** from **Wuolah** PDFs.
- [F-Secure Identity Theft Checker](https://www.f-secure.com/en/identity-theft-checker): Checks if your data appears in a **data breach**.
- [OpenSubtitles](https://www.opensubtitles.org/es): Useful if you need **subtitles**.
- [OnShape](https://www.onshape.com/en/): The CAD software I use.
- [MailTicking](https://www.mailticking.com/): **Temporary Gmail accounts** for sketchy websites.

## Essential Chrome extensions (my main browser)

- [Bypass Paywalls Chrome Clean](https://github.com/bpc-clone/bypass-paywalls-chrome-clean): Bypasses abusive paywalls on some sites.
- [Video Speed Pro](https://chromewebstore.google.com/detail/video-speed-pro/iinblfpbdoplpbdkepibimlgabgkaika): Controls playback speed on web players.
- [uBlock Origin](https://chromewebstore.google.com/detail/ublock-origin/cjpalhdlnbpafiamejdnhcphjbkeiagm): Excellent ad blocker and anti-tracking extension.
