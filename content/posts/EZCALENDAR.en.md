### Tired of making exam or event schedules by hand?

I built **EZCALENDAR** in **Python** to avoid that repetitive task. With this tool, you can generate a printable **PDF** exam schedule, and it also shows the **days remaining** until each exam.

---

## How does it work?

1. Enter exam **dates** and names.
2. Press **Enter**.
3. Automatically get an **A4 PDF** with events organized and vertically adjusted based on how many entries you have.
