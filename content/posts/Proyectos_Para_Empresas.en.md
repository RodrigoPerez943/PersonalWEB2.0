Working for **SAWY**, I developed two main tools that significantly improved the previous workflow.

---

![TEXT2QU](/PersonalWEB2.0/images/TEXT2QU.png)

### **TEXT2QU**

**TEXT2QU** is private software designed to **automate puzzle-style programming exercise generation** for online courses. It can produce **hundreds of questions in seconds**.

---

![TEXT2Q](/PersonalWEB2.0/images/TEXT2Q.png)

### **TEXT2Q**

**TEXT2Q** focuses on automatically generating **multiple-choice**, true/false, and fill-in-the-gap questions from **educational videos**.

---

Both tools were built in **Python** using **Pandas** and **OpenAI**. Generated outputs still require some **post-processing**, but the **time savings** compared to manual creation are huge.
