The logo shown in all posts and in the **About me** section indicates that this website was created by a **human**, not by a generative tool.

Although **artificial intelligence** is a powerful and useful tool, we strongly believe it should not replace **personal creation**.

> *"Human creativity has a unique value that should not be lost in an increasingly automated world."*

If you want to learn more about this initiative, you can click the **Brain Made** icon.
