A few days ago, the **glass panel support** of my home range hood broke, and my mother asked me to design a **3D-printed replacement part**. I use a **Creality V3 KE**, and it has been great.

{{< figure src="images/humo1.jpg" alt="Old parts" caption="Old Parts" class="webp" loading="lazy" width="600">}}

After measuring the original part with a caliper and iterating through a couple of prints, I got a strong and accurate fit.
