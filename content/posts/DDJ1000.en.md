After quite some time without working on any personal projects, I came across a heavily damaged Pioneer DDJ-1000 controller for a very good price, so I decided to buy it.

The jog wheels were broken, the four volume faders were snapped, and several buttons were damaged. I ordered the replacement parts and, after soldering and replacing quite a few components, it works perfectly again.

I also had to design the caps for almost all the knobs and 3D-print them. Here are some photos I took during the process.

Time to DJ!
