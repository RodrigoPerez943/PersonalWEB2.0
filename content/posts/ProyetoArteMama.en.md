My mother is a **sculptor** and wanted to simulate leaf movement using **magnets**. I helped by designing a circuit where a **stepper motor** controls a magnet with **pseudo-random** motion.

At first I considered using an **electromagnet**, but that would create a regular oscillation frequency. I preferred changing distance and rotation of a permanent magnet for a more natural motion.

A demo video will be added soon.

---

For the **stepper motor**, I designed a **magnet holder** with multiple positions. Even though we currently use two edge positions, the design allows easy changes and adjustments.
